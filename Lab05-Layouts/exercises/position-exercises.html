Exercise 5B: position relative

Exercise 6A: Overlapping Elements with Z-index

z-index: 1 (Blue — behind)
z-index: 2 (Red — middle)
z-index: 3 (Green — front)

Higher z-index = closer to the viewer. Only works on positioned elements (relative, absolute, fixed, sticky).
